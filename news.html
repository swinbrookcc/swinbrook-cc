Nets every Friday through the summer

Practice runs from 6pm at the ground every Friday until the end of August. All abilities welcome — Please drop us a message for more information using the link on the home page.

10 July 2026

The triumph of all Stars Cricket!

All Stars (5-8 years old) comes to an end with a medal ceremony and a BBQ. It has been a fantastic event every week, and everyone at Swinbrook has massively enjoyed the sessions. Please keep an eye on our Instagram for the sign up next year and for the addition of Dynamos Cricket.
