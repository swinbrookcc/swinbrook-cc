Saturday 11 July 2026 · Home vs Shipton-under-Wychwood

1st XI win on first home fixture in 2026

Swinbrook CC beat Shipton-under-Wychwood CC by 6 wickets

It was a beautiful summer's afternoon of cricket at its absolute best, with Swinbrook securing a spectacular 6-wicket victory. While the competitive edge was sharp, what truly stole the show was the fantastic, warm nature in which the entire game was played—a true credit to both clubs and local grassroots cricket. Having won the toss and elected to field, Swinbrook bowled with great precision. Rafe led the charge with a magnificent spell, finishing with fantastic figures of 3-24. Despite a hard-fought 45 from Shipton’s Finn, Swinbrook's collective fielding effort never wavered, bowling out the visitors for a competitive 175. Chasing 176 is never straightforward, especially with Shipton's opening bowlers delivering a high-quality, testing initial spell. Stepping up to the challenge was Theo, who showed immense grit to battle through the tough opening barrage. He brilliantly anchored the top order, blunting the new ball to compile a crucial, determined 31 and lay the vital platform Swinbrook needed. With the foundation set alongside a solid 33 from Tom, Ben arrived at the crease and completely shifted the momentum. Putting on a masterclass of clean hitting, Ben blasted a blistering, unbeaten 51* to take the game away from the visitors in rapid fashion. Swinbrook comfortably crossed the finish line at 176/4, wrapping up a comprehensive win with 6 wickets to spare. It was a fantastic afternoon defined by great team cohesion, superb individual performances, and wonderful camaraderie on the field!

Thanks to all who helped set up and to our umpires for giving up their afternoon.
