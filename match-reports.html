Match Reports

The week's play, written up from the boundary. Newest first.

Saturday 11 July 2026 · Home vs Leafield

Late stand sees Swinbrook home in the evening sun

Swinbrook won by 4 wickets

Leafield posted a competitive 168 all out, their opener carrying his bat for a patient fifty before the spinners worked through the middle order. In reply Swinbrook wobbled at 90 for 5 before a cool-headed sixth-wicket partnership of 74 settled matters with three overs to spare.

Teas, as ever, were outstanding. Thanks to all who helped set up and to our umpires for giving up their afternoon.

Saturday 4 July 2026 · Away vs Chadlington

Rain has the final say at Chadlington

Match abandoned — rain

Twenty-two overs of promising cricket were cut short by a band of weather that arrived from the west and refused to leave. Swinbrook were 84 for 2 at the interruption. The fixture will not be rearranged.
